About Baitul Asl

Baitul Asl, which means "House of Honey", is a family-owned business dedicated to providing the highest quality raw honey products. Our honey is sourced directly from local beekeepers who practice sustainable beekeeping methods.

We believe in the power of natural, unprocessed honey to promote health and wellness. Each jar of our honey is carefully selected to ensure purity, flavor, and maximum nutritional benefits.

From our classic raw honey to specialty infused varieties, we offer a diverse range of products to suit every taste and need.
